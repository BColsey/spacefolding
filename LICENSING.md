# Licensing

Spacefolding is licensed under the **Functional Source License 1.1, ALv2**
([FSL-1.1-ALv2](./LICENSE)) — SPDX identifier `FSL-1.1-ALv2`.

FSL is a "mostly permissive non-compete" license (created by Sentry). In plain
terms:

**Free — no license required (Permitted Purposes):**
- Internal use and access.
- Non-commercial education and research.
- Professional services you provide to someone already using Spacefolding.

**Requires a commercial license — a "Competing Use":**
- Making Spacefolding available to others in a commercial product or service
  that (1) substitutes for Spacefolding, (2) substitutes for another product or
  service we offer, or (3) offers the same or substantially similar
  functionality. This is what the commercial license exists to cover.

**Future license:** on the second anniversary of each release's availability,
that release automatically also becomes available under **Apache-2.0**. So every
release becomes fully permissive two years after it ships.

## Obtaining a commercial license

For a commercial license permitting a Competing Use (e.g. embedding Spacefolding
in a commercial product or hosted service), contact the copyright holder:

> **Commercial licensing contact is pending at first publish; until set, reach
> the copyright holder via the repository** — https://github.com/BColsey/spacefolding
> (open an issue or use the Security contact). A `commercial@<domain>` address
> and/or a self-serve purchase link will be added when monetization operates.

Until then, reach the copyright holder via the repository:
https://github.com/BColsey/spacefolding

## Why FSL

FSL enables monetization without free-riding: developers can use, study, and
build on Spacefolding freely, but a competitor cannot resell it or host it as a
competing service without a commercial license. The automatic conversion to
Apache-2.0 after two years keeps the project contributor- and community-friendly.
Rationale and alternatives considered are in
[`docs/decisions/adr-002-license-fsl.md`](./docs/decisions/adr-002-license-fsl.md).
